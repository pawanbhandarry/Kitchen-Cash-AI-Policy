Request Account Deletion

Users of Kitchen Cash AI may request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Account information
  • Email address
  • Uploaded kitchen images
  • Inventory records
  • Recipe history
  • Savings and waste tracking records
  • Application preferences

What Data May Be Retained

Certain records may be retained when required by law, fraud prevention requirements, or billing obligations.

How To Request Deletion

Send an email to:

support@yourdomain.com

Include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Subject line: Delete My Account

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.
